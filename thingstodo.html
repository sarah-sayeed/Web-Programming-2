Desert Safari & Dune Bashing

Experience the thrill of 4x4 dune bashing across the golden sands, followed by a traditional Bedouin camp dinner under the stars.

Jebel Jais Flight Zipline
📍 Ras Al Khaimah
Fly across the world's longest zipline at over 1,600 metres above sea level, with jaw-dropping views of the Hajar Mountains.

Louvre Abu Dhabi
📍 Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi
Explore one of the world's most iconic museums, home to thousands of artworks spanning human history, housed in a stunning waterfront building.
⏱ 2–4 Hours 👥 All Ages

Al Bidyah Mosque
📍 Fujairah
Visit the UAE's oldest mosque, dating back to 1446. The simple mud-brick structure sits against a dramatic mountain backdrop.
⏱ 1 Hour 👥 All Ages
Culture 📷 View Gallery
Ajman Museum
📍 Ajman
Housed in an 18th-century fort, Ajman Museum gives a fascinating window into the emirate's past — from traditional weapons to archaeological finds.
⏱ 1–2 Hours 👥 All Ages
Beach & Water 📷 View Gallery
Snorkelling at Snoopy Island
📍 Al Aqah, Fujairah
Fujairah's east coast waters are some of the clearest in the UAE. Snoopy Island is famous for its coral reefs and colourful marine life.
⏱ Half Day 👥 All Ages
Family 📷 View Gallery
Wild Wadi Waterpark
📍 Jumeirah, Dubai
For over two decades of adventure, Wild Wadi has been Dubai’s go-to for splash-tastic fun, blending adrenaline rides and creating unforgettable memories every visit.
⏱ Full Day 👥 Families

Aquaventure Waterpark
📍 Atlantis The Palm, Dubai
One of the world's best waterparks with over 105 slides and rides, a private beach, and a lazy river. The ultimate family day out.
⏱ Full Day 👥 Families
