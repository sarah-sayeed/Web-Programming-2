Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Holidae is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

What We Collect

When you submit a booking enquiry through our website, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Travel dates, destination preferences, and guest numbers
  • Any special requests you provide in the message field

How We Use It

We use your information solely to process and confirm your holiday booking. We may contact you by email or phone to follow up on your enquiry. We do not sell, share, or pass your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for the site to function correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Data Retention

Booking enquiry data is retained for up to 12 months after your travel date, after which it is securely deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at bookings@holidae.ae.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, email us at bookings@holidae.ae and we will respond within 5 working days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact our team at bookings@holidae.ae or call +971 4 288 3190.
